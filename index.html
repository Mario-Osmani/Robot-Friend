Robot Friend

About RoboPage:

Welcome to RoboPage, where you can interact with our friendly robot friend. Hover over different parts of the robot to see them come to life. Each part has a unique animation. Have fun exploring!

Hover Instructions:

  • Head:The robot's head grows to show its excitement and spins around like it's dancing at a party, and its eyes turn red from all the fun.
  • Torso: The robot's torso is having a blast! Its right arm does the robot twist clockwise while the left arm busts some moves by twisting counterclockwise.
  • Right Arm: The robot's right arm loves to show off! It does a fancy clockwise spin and changes color to impress everyone.
  • Left Arm: The robot's left arm is the life of the party! It spins counterclockwise and changes color to join in on the fun.
  • Left Leg: The robot's left leg enjoys some fancy footwork, gracefully rotating clockwise, as if it's showing off some dance moves.
  • Right Leg: The robot's right leg is a bit rebellious, spinning counterclockwise, but it always stays in line with the groove.
